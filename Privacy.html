Privacy Policy for SeeLocal

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

At SeeLocal, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of data:

Personal Information: Name, email address, username, profile picture, and selected user role (e.g., customer, tour guide).

Tour & Event Data: Created tours, favorites, joined events, and booking activity.

Authentication: Secure login credentials or third-party tokens (e.g., Google sign-in).

Device & Usage Info: Device type, crash logs, app performance metrics, and usage behavior.

Note: We do not currently collect or process payment information. Stripe payment features may be added in future versions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Create and manage your SeeLocal profile
  • Display tours and suggested content
  • Enable features like friend recommendations, bookings, and messaging
  • Improve app performance, security, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share it:

  • With other users (e.g., to display your name and profile in bookings)
  • With trusted providers (e.g., Firebase for hosting)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

4. Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Hashed passwords
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Limited access to user data

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • View, update, or delete your profile data at any time
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us at Sales@seelocal.ca
  • Disable push notifications via your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

SeeLocal is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If discovered, such accounts will be removed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All changes will be communicated within the app or via email.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact:

Email: Sales@seelocal.ca
